What is the BexleyMesh Project?

BexleyMesh is a community-built, off-grid communication network for the Bexley, Ohio area. We use affordable, low-power LoRa radios running MeshCore firmware to send text messages and share GPS locations — no cell towers, no internet, 100% free of cost.

Our solar-powered repeater nodes relay messages across the area automatically, creating a resilient mesh that keeps working when traditional infrastructure goes down. Whether you're preparing for emergencies, exploring radio technology, or just want to stay connected off-grid — getting started takes only a few minutes.
